Truss Design Project - Static Mechanics

Description

For my Static Mechanics class, I collaborated with several classmates to design, analyze, and optimize a 2D straw truss. For this project, straws were first tested to determine a relationship between length and buckling weight. Considering this relationship, my group designed several different 2D trusses with the goal of creating the strongest truss at the smallest cost. I then developed a matlab script to determine the max load sustained, the failing member, the cost, and the force in each member for each truss design. Using this program, several more designs were made to optimize the load to cost ratio and the final design was eventually constructed and tested.

My Role in the Project

  • Developed a Matlab script to determine the forces in each member and the other statistics mentioned above
  • Contributed to the optimization of the design and the construction of the truss
  • Organized meetings and planned schedules and deadlines for the semester long project

Tools/Languages Used

  • Matlab
  • Microsoft Excel

Further Information
